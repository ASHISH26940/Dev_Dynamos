No to ragging


Ragging is a disturbing reality in the higher education system of our country. Despite the fact that over the years ragging has claimed hundreds of innocent lives and has ruined careers of thousands of bright students, the practice is still perceived by many as a way of ‘familiarization’ and an ‘initiation into the real world’ for young college-going students. Other organisations/bodies working in this field have also attempted to define ragging, the variety of definitions being reflective of differences in perspective.

About Us

Welcome to our portal, a safe haven against the damaging impact of ragging. As newcomers, first-year students often find themselves lost and unsure of where to turn when faced with troubling situations. This uncertainty can lead to silence and suffering, potentially leaving lasting scars on their college journey.

Our platform was born from the need to provide a reliable outlet for students to voice their concerns without the struggle of finding the right person to confide in. We're here to aid the Anti-ragging Committee in swiftly addressing issues, ensuring timely action and support.

What's in store:

  • Complaint Form: A simple form for students to register complaints. Just your name, class, department, and roll number – we've got the rest covered.
  • Anonymous Form: For those who wish to be unsung heroes, a form to report incidents without revealing your identity. Extend a helping hand to those who can't speak up.

Our mission is crystal clear: wipe out ragging in all its forms, fostering a secure atmosphere for first-year students. Prevention is our priority, backed by the weight of College Regulations to penalize wrongdoers.

Join us in making the campus a haven for learning and growth, free from the shadows of ragging.
